  <h2 id="getting-started">Getting Started</h2>

  <h3 id="downloading-mignori">Downloading Mignori</h3>
  <p>You can download Mignori directly from the App Store using the official link.</p>

  <h3 id="first-launch">First Launch & Initial Setup</h3>
  <p>When you first open the app, it will automatically guide you through the basic setup.</p>

  <h3 id="configuring-mignori">Configuring Mignori</h3>
  <p>Open the Settings tab to customize folders, appearance, and performance preferences.</p>

  <hr />

  <h2 id="using-the-app">Using the App</h2>

  <h3 id="managing-folders">Managing Folders</h3>
  <p>Mignori automatically groups your images by folder structure, but you can also create custom collections.</p>

  <h3 id="sorting-and-filters">Sorting & Filters</h3>
  <p>You can sort by name, file size, creation date, or apply metadata-based filters.</p>

  <h3 id="metadata-extraction">Image Metadata Extraction</h3>
  <p>Mignori extracts EXIF, AI metadata, and SD prompts when available.</p>

  <hr />

  <h2 id="premium">Premium Features</h2>

  <h3 id="is-premium-worth-it">Is Premium worth it?</h3>
  <p>If you handle thousands of images, Premium unlocks powerful batch tools and performance options.</p>

  <h3 id="pricing">Pricing</h3>
  <p>Mignori offers a one-time purchase with optional add-ons.</p>

  <h3 id="family-sharing">Family Sharing</h3>
  <p>Premium purchases support Apple's built-in family sharing.</p>

  <hr />

  <h2 id="privacy">Privacy & Security</h2>

  <h3 id="data-storage">Where is my data stored?</h3>
  <p>All image analysis happens locally. Nothing is uploaded to servers.</p>

  <h3 id="permissions">What permissions are required?</h3>
  <p>Mignori only requires access to local files if you choose to import them.</p>

  <h3 id="analytics">Do you collect analytics?</h3>
  <p>Anonymous crash data may be collected via Apple if you opt in.</p>

  <hr />

  <h2 id="troubleshooting">Troubleshooting</h2>

  <h3 id="not-scanning">Mignori is not scanning images</h3>
  <p>Ensure the folder path exists and you have read permissions.</p>

  <h3 id="slow-performance">Slow performance</h3>
  <p>Large imported folders can take time to analyze; you can enable performance mode in settings.</p>

  <h3 id="contact-support">How to contact support</h3>
  <p>Use the in-app Support button or reach us through the official website.</p>
