Mignori started as a simple experiment: *what if booru browsing could feel like a native iOS experience?*

Most existing apps are web wrappers, slow, outdated, or simply don’t respect the design conventions of iOS 26.

In this article we’ll explore the journey from prototype to early beta, what makes Mignori unique, and what’s coming next.
