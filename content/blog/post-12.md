With every major release, Swift becomes more powerful, expressive, and safe. Swift 6.0 is no exception, with a strong focus on refining the concurrency model introduced in Swift 5.5.

This post highlights our favorite new features, including improved actor isolation, new tools for structured concurrency, and powerful additions to the generics system that will change how we write reusable code.
