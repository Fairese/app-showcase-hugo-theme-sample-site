Building a fantastic app is only half the battle. Getting it into the hands of users requires a different set of skills: marketing.

This doesn't have to mean expensive ad campaigns. We'll cover grassroots marketing techniques, the basics of App Store Optimization (ASO), and how to leverage social media to build a community around your product.
