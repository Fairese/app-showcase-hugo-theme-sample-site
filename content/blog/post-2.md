The Fairese App Showcase theme was born from a simple goal:
create a theme modern enough for 2025, yet simple enough that any
indie developer could deploy it without fighting the tooling.

In this post we break down some design decisions, why we avoided
JavaScript wherever possible, and how to extend the theme to match your brand.
